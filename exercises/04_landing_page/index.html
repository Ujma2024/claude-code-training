What It Does

Intelligence Built Into Every Degree

Four powerful features working together to keep your home perfectly comfortable while saving energy.

🧠

Adaptive Learning

Studies your daily routine for the first week, then automatically adjusts temperatures based on when you wake up, leave home, and go to sleep. No manual scheduling needed.

⛅

Weather-Aware

Connects to local weather data and pre-adjusts your heating or cooling before temperature swings hit. If a cold front is coming tonight, your home is already warming up.

🏠

Room-by-Room Control

Pair with ThermoSmart sensors to control each room independently. No more heating the guest room nobody uses or freezing in your home office.

📊

Energy Dashboard

Track your energy usage in real time through the ThermoSmart app. See daily, weekly, and monthly reports with clear breakdowns of where your energy goes.
